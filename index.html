About Me

A Brief Summary

I am a May' 2014 passout for Bachelors in Computer Science Engineering, working as a Front-end Developer at Moonraft Innovation Labs, Bangalore since April' 2018.

  • Current Employer - Moonraft Innovation Labs, Bengaluru
  • Job Role - Front-end Developer
  • Experience - 5 years

I've been working as a UI Developer since March 2016. At present, I am part of a Global Design and Customer Experience firm in Bangalore, building a product for the hospitality industry in India. Ionic, CosmicJS, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Firebase,Cordova, etc. are few of the technologies/ frameworks that I am currently working with. Previously my job required me to handle L1-L2 administration and development of Microsoft SharePoint and Microsoft SQL Server infrastructures. Moving ahead, I have been involved in end to end creation of some intranet websites - from development to deployment. I have also contributed in front-end design and development of dashboards for few intra-organization portals. Later, before stepping into UI development, I worked in a CI/CD project which required hands on knowledge of GIT, JIRA, Jenkins, etc.


In my free time, you can find me either engrossed in my laptop, sucking on all that I can about geeky-tech stuff, or lost into another world while playing guitar! (I am a big time music buff!)


I occasionally indulge in online gaming (Counter-Strike, Guns of Boom, PUBG). My other interests lie in MARVEL and DC comics, sketching, making portraits, blogging, playing pool and table tennis, surfing Quora or 9gag'ging!!
